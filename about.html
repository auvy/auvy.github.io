WE LOVE CARS
BEGINNINGS "Hillclimb Monsters" began its journey as a humble Facebook page, driven by our shared enthusiasm for the exhilarating world of hillclimb racing. It all started with a simple mission: to curate and share captivating videos showcasing the raw power and agility of hillclimb cars tearing through challenging courses. As the page gained traction and followers, it became evident that our passion for this niche motorsport resonated deeply within the automotive community.
YOUTUBE With the rise of video-sharing platforms like YouTube, "Hillclimb Monsters" expanded our reach and influence, establishing a significant presence in the digital realm. Our YouTube channel quickly became a go-to destination for enthusiasts seeking adrenaline-pumping content featuring the most awe-inspiring hillclimb machines in action. Through meticulously crafted videos and engaging storytelling, we captivated audiences worldwide, further solidifying our status as a leading authority in the realm of hillclimb racing.
EVENTS However, "Hillclimb Monsters" didn't stop at just online platforms. Recognizing our growing influence and the potential to make an even greater impact, we began forging partnerships with renowned automotive journals and motorsport event organizers. This strategic collaboration allowed us to diversify our content offerings and reach a broader audience while also gaining access to exclusive events and insider insights.

OUR PARTNERS
We frequently partner up with autosport event organizer companies, such as Pikes Peak, Trento-Bondone, Goodwood and many more.
